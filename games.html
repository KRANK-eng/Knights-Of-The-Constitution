The KOTC isnt just a Helldivers 2 server

We are mainly a Helldivers 2 server but, we play many other games as well. We stay active so if you ever need a friend for a certain game there is probally someone who will join you! I will list some of the other High light games we do

Minecraft

This community also plays Minecraft quite frequently having a few servers that we play on. We stay active on the servers and we try our best to keep everyone happy with the server and keep interest in it.

Ready Or Not

Ready Or Not is a tactical first-person shooter that emphasizes teamwork and strategy. Our community enjoys coordinating operations and working together to achieve objectives.

Battlefront

Battlefront is a series of games set in the Star Wars universe, focusing on large-scale battles between the Galactic Empire and the Rebel Alliance. Our community enjoys reliving epic space battles and ground assaults.

Deep Rock Galactic

Deep Rock Galactic is a cooperative first-person shooter featuring a team of dwarves on dangerous missions in procedurally generated caves. Our community enjoys working together to complete objectives and fend off alien threats.

Warhammer 40k

Warhammer 40k is a tabletop wargame set in a dystopian future where various factions battle for supremacy. Our community enjoys discussing lore, strategies, and participating in tabletop battles. And also playing the video games like Space Marine 2

Dungeons and Dragons

Dungeons and Dragons is a tabletop role-playing game that encourages creativity and teamwork. Our community enjoys embarking on epic quests, battling fearsome monsters, and crafting intricate stories together.

Monster Hunter

Monster Hunter is a series of action role-playing games that focus on hunting down and defeating large creatures in various environments. Our community enjoys teaming up to take on challenging hunts and share their experiences.

We play monster hunter Wilds mainly but we do have some monster hunter world plays and maybe some rise players.

Sea of Thieves

Sea of Thieves is a multiplayer pirate adventure game where players explore an open world, engage in ship battles, and search for treasure. Our community enjoys forming crews and embarking on epic voyages across the seas.

No Mans Sky

No Mans Sky is an open-world survival game set in a procedurally generated universe. Players can explore planets, gather resources, and build bases. Our community enjoys collaborating on exploration missions and sharing discoveries.
