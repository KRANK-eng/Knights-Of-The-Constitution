Houses Of The KOTC

Each house has its own unique role and responsibilities within the KOTC, contributing to the overall strength and effectiveness of our faction.

The Obliterator House

The Obliterator House is known for its heavy firepower and relentless assault tactics. Members of this house are trained to use the most powerful weapons available, ensuring that they can take down any enemy that stands in their way.

They are lead by Charger who Is part of Walmarts high council. Charger is the embodiement of a obliterator destroying everything in his path with his heavy weapons and brute strength, he also inspires all the other obliterators to keep on going through the battle.

Their special tactics are lighting up the battlefeild with fire, napalm, and explosives.

The Kitchen

The Kitchen is the obliterator's Special Ops team. They are lead by Head Chef KRANK. Head Chef KRANK is a skilled warrior, he fights through the battlefeild by cooking his enemies from the inside out. Head Chef KRANK hand picks the members of the kitchen only choosing the finest of warriors.

The Apothecary House

The Apothecary House focuses on support and healing, ensuring that their allies can continue fighting even in the toughest battles. They are equipped with advanced medical technology and are trained to keep their comrades alive.

They are lead by Smollet another member of Walmarts high council. Smollet has proved to be a very valuable member to the Knights of the constitution and will remain a friend to many

Their special tactics are healing every ally on the battlefeild that needs help,but dont let them fool you they are still amazing fighters on the battlefeild and use all our weapons to the fullest.

The Sicarian House

The Sicarian House is known for its stealth and precision strikes. Members of this house are trained to infiltrate enemy lines and take out key targets with deadly efficiency. They excel in reconnaissance and sabotage missions.

They are lead by Phil the temporary sicarian leader

Their special tactics are stealth and precision strikes, they can take out key targets with deadly efficiency.

The Silver Angels House

The Silver Angels are the engineers of the KOTC, specializing in advanced technology and weaponry. They are responsible for maintaining and upgrading the faction's equipment, ensuring that their allies have the best tools for battle.

The Silver Angels do not have a leader at the moment

Their special tactics are to use advanced technology and weaponry to support their allies, they can also repair equipment on the battlefield. And spearhead all the manufacturing and tech upgrades of the KOTC

The Templars House

The Templar House specializes in close-quarters combat and honour. They are not afraid to throw hands when need be

They are lead by Crusader,who is a member of Walmarts high council. Crusader is the best fit for the leader of the templars as he always charges head first into the battle with his melee weapon in hand charging for Democracy!

Their special tactics are to use their combat skills to take down enemies in close quarters, they are also trained to fight with honour and respect.

The Ambassadors

The Ambassadors are responsible for diplomacy and communication within the KOTC. They work to maintain relationships with other factions and ensure that the voice of the KOTC is heard.

They are lead by Spartan Knight who is Lord Ambassador. Spartan has remained a good friend to all and is the best fit for being the lord ambassador for being a good person to talk to and he is very convincing.
